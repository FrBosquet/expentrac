# Hello world!

This is a test post to check that everything is working properly

Expentrac allows you to track your expenses and income, and provides you with a clear overview of your financial situation. It supports budgeting, multiple accounts, and multiple currencies. It imports bank statements from the major Irish banks, and automatically categorises your expenses. On top of that, it can generate reports and analyse your spending habits.

- Track your expenses and income
- Get a clear overview of your financial situation
- Manage your budget
- Manage your bank accounts
- Manage your credit cards
- Manage your loans
